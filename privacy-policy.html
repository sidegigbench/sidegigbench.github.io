1. Information We Collect

User-Provided Data

The App allows users to voluntarily provide and store barcode-related information, including:

  • barcodes scanned using the device camera,
  • barcodes extracted from images selected by the user from their photo library,
  • barcode information manually entered by the user.

This information is referred to as user-generated content.

We do not verify, validate, or authenticate the accuracy or legitimacy of user-provided barcode data.

2. How We Use Information

User-provided barcode data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App, including:

  • storing barcode-based cards,
  • organizing and displaying stored cards,
  • enabling convenient access to barcodes for personal use.

We do not use user data for:

  • advertising,
  • analytics,
  • profiling,
  • tracking across apps or websites.

3. Data Storage

All barcode data is stored locally on the user's device.

We do not upload, transmit, or store barcode data, photos, or images on our servers.

If a user deletes a stored barcode or card within the App, the data is permanently removed from the App's local storage.

4. Permissions

The App may request access to the following device features:

Camera Access

Used exclusively to scan barcodes in real time when the user initiates a scan.

Photo Library Access

Used only to allow users to select specific images containing barcodes for scanning.

Users may choose:

  • limited photo access (selected images only), or
  • full photo library access (optional).

The App does not access photos, camera data, or other content without explicit user action and does not process such data in the background.

Permissions can be revoked at any time through the device's system settings.
